Vertical & Interior Garden

Vertical garden

Isn’t it so cool to be in a space with vertically oriented high-density plantings? Vertical gardening, use structures or columnar trees to create garden rooms. Vertical gardens are grown on walls, windows of houses, offices etc. where they don’t have a garden space outside their place.

Trellises, bound to the grounds or large containers allow you to grow vines, flowers, and even vegetables in a vertical garden, with minimal space than traditional gardening requirements.

Zen Garden

Peace and tranquility is the symbol of Zen garden. These are carefully controlled settings of raked sand or rocks and precisely clipped shrubs. Zen gardens are used for meditation and calmness.

FRAGALA has meticulously observed the Japanese Zen gardens design styles and trained their landscapers. These gardens are used as a way to try to reach enlightenment. It improves your focus and develops a sense of well-being. Even if you not fond of meditation and Zen, rock gardens with plants can still offer a relaxing and beautiful space in the yard.
